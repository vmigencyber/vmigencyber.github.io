About the Program

Join us for an exciting summer camp at Virginia Military Institute! Funded by the National Security Agency and organized by the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at VMI.

GenCyber is a national initiative with the following goals:

  • Ignite, sustain, and increase awareness of K-12 cybersecurity content and cybersecurity postsecondary and career opportunities for participants.
  • Increase student diversity in cybersecurity college and career readiness pathways at the K-12 level.
  • Facilitate teacher readiness within a teacher learning community to learn, develop, and deliver cybersecurity content for the K-12 classroom in collaboration with other nationwide initiatives.
